How can I help you in your business?

I am a generalist designer which means that I have knowledge and experience in various areas of UI/UX design. As a business owner, hiring generalists allows you to have a smaller workforce, as they can do everything from researching and designing to testing. Additionally, having generalists working on different projects means that there is less of a need to hand off projects between different departments, which can be helpful when it comes to user research and design.

Hi, It's me again

My journey in UI/UX is out of the ordinary, I never thought I would be a designer. Here is how it goes.

I studied Biology back in college. During my last years, I realized that I don't want to pursue it as a career. Then, I bumped into the graphic designing field and from there came to know about the user interface and user experience. I LOVED IT IMMEDIATELY!

UI/UX was an utmost fascination to me. I started to learn the required skills and tools online. It helped me build my first portfolio on Behance (not so great I admit) and land an internship. From there I got the amazing opportunity to work at System Plus. I have over two years of experience in the field now. I am very focused on my learning and have been doing online courses to cover my knowledge gap.

I never knew design could be so powerful. I enjoy designing interfaces that users appreciate and find easy to use. After working on different projects, I have realized that my interest lies particularly in the visual design area.
